Biography

2020.9-        Department of Curriculum and Learning Sciences         Zhejiang University           Ph.D. Candidate
2022.6-2023.5     Department of Educational Psychology             The University of Tokyo     Special Research Student

Enming ZHANG studied vehicle engineering at Jilin University and got his bachelor's degree in 2018. Then he was accepted by the College of Education, Zhejiang University, and studied curriculum and instruction as a graduate student. Now he is studying as a special research student in the Department of Educational Psychology, The University of Tokyo, granted by China Scholarship Council (CSC). He had authored over 10 publications in scientific journals and books (some of them were translating articles and books). As for the research area, he is mainly interested in learning from errors, self-regulated learning, and teacher judgments. He is also devoted to connecting the implications of educational psychology research with school practices.

Enming ZHANG would like to cooperate with researchers and teachers worldwide to manipulate empirical research on teaching and learning improvement. You can contact him by email: definedming at zju.edu.cn

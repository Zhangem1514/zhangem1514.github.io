Biography

2018.9-        Department of Curriculum and Learning Sciences         Zhejiang University           Ph.D. Candidate
2022.6-2023.6     Department of Educational Psychology             The University of Tokyo     Special Research Student

Enming ZHANG studied vehicle engineering at Jilin University and got his bachelor's degree in 2018. Now he is a Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Curriculum and Learning Sciences, Zhejiang University (Successive Postgraduate and Doctoral Programs, 2018-). He also has an experience as a special research student studying at the Department of Educational Psychology, The University of Tokyo (2022-2023), granted by China Scholarship Council (CSC). He had authored over 10 publications in scientific journals and books (some of them were translating articles and books). His research interests primarily fall in educational psychology and its connection with school practices, including the topics of learning from errors, self-regulated learning, and teacher judgments.

Enming ZHANG would like to cooperate with researchers and teachers worldwide to manipulate empirical research on teaching and learning improvement. You can contact him by email: definedming at zju.edu.cn
